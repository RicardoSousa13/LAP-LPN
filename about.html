LPN

Luís Pinto Nunes (Porto, 1988) has a degree in Visual Arts – Painting, and post-graduation on Art Studies – Museology and Curatorial Studies at the Faculty of Fine Arts, University of Porto (FBAUP). Enroled the Independent Study Programme of Escola Maumaus. Coordinator of the Museum and Exhibitions Office at the Faculty of Fine Arts, University of Porto, developing museological and curatorial projects. Committee member and curator of xCoAx – Computation Communication Aesthetics and X, and member of the acquisitions committee for artworks for the collection of Porto City Hall (Pláka) in 2018-19. 

LAP

Luís Albuquerque Pinho (Porto, 1978) has a degree in Architecture from Universidade Lusíada in Porto, in 2001. Collaborated with Isabel Furtado and João Pedro Serôdio, at Serôdio Furtado & associados, between 2000 and 2011. Associated Architect at Garcia e Albuquerque. Post-graduation on Art Studies – Museum and Curatorial Studies at the Faculty of Fine Arts, University of Porto (FBAUP). Resident curator at Node Center Berlin. Enroled the Independent Study Programme of Escola Maumaus at Guimarães European City of Culture. Co-curated Wunderblock —Traces of Process, Documents and Memory, at the Grimmuseum, Berlin (2011).  In 2014, he attended the seminar L’emoció no diu “jo”, taught by Georges Didi-Huberman, as part of the Independent Studies Program of the Museu d’Art Contemporani de Barcelona (MACBA). Country farmer, funded his own office Albuquerque Pinho Arquitectos
